X-Bay mini with mini jack interface

Mini jack screw adapter

A handy adapter for when things don't fit. You can cut wires on one side and tighten them in the screw termination.

Relay X-Mod

Plug this X-Mod in X-Bay and you can connect channels to a 5 volt supply channel. The relay can switch high voltage AC. An obvious use case is home domotica. Setting this up was remarkable easy. No extra software updates are needed.

RCA to Pin

A one off RCA to pin header adapter. The cables are cut in half, stripped and plugged in to the spring loader block.
